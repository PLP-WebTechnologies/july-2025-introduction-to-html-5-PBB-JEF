About Semantic Structure

What is Semantic HTML?

Semantic HTML5 uses meaningful tags like <header>, <article>, and <footer> to structure content. This helps browsers, screen readers, and search engines understand your page better.

Why It Matters

Semantic structure improves accessibility for assistive technologies and enhances SEO by clarifying your page's content and hierarchy.

Pro Tip

Use headings in logical order (e.g., h1 → h2 → h3) and provide alt text for images to make your site screen-reader friendly.

Core Principles of Good Web Design

Accessibility

Ensure your page is usable for all, including those using screen readers or keyboard-only navigation.

SEO Best Practices

Use descriptive headings, meta tags, and well-structured content to help search engines index your site effectively.
